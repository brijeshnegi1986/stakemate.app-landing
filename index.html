Support

We're here to help.

Contact Us

For any questions, issues, or feedback, email us at support@stakemate.com.au and we'll get back to you within 1–2 business days.

Manage Your Subscription

PokerRoll Pro subscriptions are managed through Apple. To cancel or change your subscription:

  • Open Settings on your iPhone
  • Tap your name → Subscriptions
  • Select PokerRoll and manage from there

Restore Purchases

If you've reinstalled the app or switched devices, go to Profile → Upgrade to Pro and tap Restore Purchases.

Delete Your Account

You can request account deletion at any time from the Profile screen inside the app. Local session data can be cleared from Game Settings → Data Management.

Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2025

PokerRoll is built with your privacy in mind. This policy explains what data we collect, why, and how it is used.

Data We Collect

  • Email address — only if you sign in with Apple or Google
  • Display name — only if you choose to set one in your profile
  • Session and hand data you enter — stored locally on your device
  • Subscription and purchase status — managed securely by Apple and RevenueCat

How We Use Your Data

  • To provide and improve the PokerRoll app
  • To manage your subscription and restore purchases
  • To send optional emails about challenges and prize opportunities (only if you opt in)

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We use the following trusted third-party services:

  • Supabase — authentication and optional cloud sync
  • RevenueCat — subscription management
  • Apple StoreKit — in-app purchases

Data Retention

Session data is stored locally on your device and is under your full control. If you create an account, your profile data is stored securely on our servers and deleted upon request.

Your Rights

  • Request deletion of your account and all data from the Profile screen in the app
  • Opt out of promotional emails at any time from your profile settings
  • Clear all local session data from Game Settings at any time

Children's Privacy

PokerRoll is not directed at children under 18. We do not knowingly collect data from minors.
